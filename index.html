About Me

I was first interested in getting into IT because of my dad, who works as a software engineer himself. Upon taking a basic computer science class in high school, I discovered a profound passion for the subject that compelled me to later take every other computer-related course my highschool had to offer during my other two years there. Since then, I've known that it was the perfect career path for me.

Currently I'm freshly graduated from college with a technical degree in Software Development looking to begin my path to becoming a master software engineer. I hope that every professional encounter I have will be an opportunity to bring me closer to my career goals.
